Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register for an event?

Go to the "Events" page, click on your chosen event, and fill out the registration form.

2. Can I cancel my registration?

Yes. Log into your account, go to "My Events", and click "Cancel Registration".

3. How do I volunteer for an event?

Visit the "Volunteer" section and submit your interest form. You'll be contacted by organizers.

4. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
